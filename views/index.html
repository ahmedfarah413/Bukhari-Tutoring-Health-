Welcome to Bukhari Tutoring!!!

Bukhari Tutoring & Health is a nonprofit STEM Based tutoring & mentorship organization striving to promote academic success. Our mission is to mentor students who are subject to academic failure, crime, drug abuse, etc... And transform them into academically successful, morally upright, and admirable individuals who bring true value to our communities.

Our Goal

Here at Bukhari Tutoring, our main goal is to provide a safe and influential enviornment for the next generations to come and grow, even with the tough surroundings for most children in our area. Here at Bukhari Tutoring, our tutors dedicate themselves to attend to all of our student's homework help, teaching and assisting in learning valuable future skills, and providing a fun and safe enviornment for the student to grow with us. Nearly 81% of high school students in Atlanta fail to receive a bachelor degree, but here at Bukhari Tutoring, we need your support to spark the academic revolution that will uplift our community.
