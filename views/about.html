About Us

How Do We Help With Your Child?

We do this by personalizing one-on-one tutoring sessions to bring out the very best from each student.With over 15 years of combined tutoring experience throughout our team, we offer tutoring in nearly every subject: Language Arts, Algebra, Calculus, Chemistry, Biology, Physics, History, Arabic, and much more.


In addition to our tutoring programs, we offer skill development programs that help students to enter the workforce with a head start and/or become successful entrepreneurs. We are also very involved in our community hosting basketball and soccer clinics to help students stay health and active.


Through these services we bring awareness to the harms of drugs & crime and the importance of receiving an education. Bukhari Tutoring & Health is the solution to rectifying the future of our youth.
